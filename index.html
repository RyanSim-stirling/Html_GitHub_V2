Tale of Samuel Shaw

In 2010, a student named Samuel Shaw was found dead in one of the old campus bathrooms. Official reports say
it was an overdose - methamphetamine. They said he was using it to cope with the intense pressure of school,
two part-time jobs, and a fractured home life. But the story didn’t end there.


Ever since his death, strange things have been happening in that bathroom. A sharp, bitter chemical smell clings to the air..... like something artificial, burning. And then there’s the laughter: soft, breathy, and wrong.

It doesn’t sound joyful .... it sounds like someone laughing to themselves in the dark, the mad laughter of someone who has snapped. Some say that you can hear it from outside, even when the bathroom is empty and if you try to enter, the laughter stops… but not all the time. Occasionally, the laughter lingers, echoing off the tiles getting louder and louder....

There's a rumor that one janitor quit without finishing their shift after unlocking that stall during a routine cleaning. They never said what they saw. Just left the keys on the floor and walked out.

Some say his spirit is stuck, a result of his untimely death. Others think he came back to spread his sorrow to the living. Either way, no one uses that bathroom after sunset.... Not unless they want to hear the bathroom laughing back.
